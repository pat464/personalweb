Network Design and Security

I am involved in the strategic planning and arrangement of digital communication pathways, devices, and protocols to optimize data flow, accessibility, and efficiency. Additionally, this involves configuring components like routers, switches, and servers to ensure reliable connectivity and resource allocation. Network security involves implementing measures to safeguard these systems from unauthorized access, data breaches, and cyber threats.
